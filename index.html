Location

Our wedding will be held at Cabin Creek Lodge in Easton, Washington, 20 minutes East of Snoqualmie Pass on I-90. Travel time from Seattle is about an hour and 15 minutes.

From I-90 Eastbound, take Exit 63 for U-Fish Road. Turn right on U-Fish Road (unpaved) and continue 2.2 miles until you see a sign on the right for Cabin Creek Lodge. Follow the entrance road another half mile to the lodge, where you will find parking.
